Hello and welcome to Washington Wellness for your health and wellness needs. The health and fitness industry is a very large community. There's a lot of information out there and we're here to point you in the right direction to progress your health and wellness goals.

Marcus Washington has been a certified fitness professional since Jan 2004. Marcus carries five nationally recognized certifications NASM, NSCA, ACE, NESTA, CSCS.

He is a former two time All American and had a short professional track and field career that was cut short by injuries. This motivated Marcus to pursue rehabilitation work and correctional exercise.

After a short stint at UT Southwestern Marcus pursued his career in the fitness industry and is now one of the leading experts in the following: workout, strength training, strength and conditioning, boxing and kickboxing, HIIT training (high intensity interval training) A.R.T (Active release techniques) Core training.

Marcus connects with the client mentally and this helps him develop the physical well being. Exercise programs are tailored to the client's needs and follow a six week program design that includes modifications along the journey to a better physical well being.

Marcus brings a lot of passion and accountability to every client he engages with and works to bring the best out of their health and fitness. This is done simply by designing a program regime that will fit the client's need and the goal perceived will eventually be achieved.
