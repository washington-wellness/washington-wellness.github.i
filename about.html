About

Marcus Washington is a certified trainer and strength and conditioning coach with a background in correctional exercises. Marcus' experience dates back to Jan 2004 when he first started his career at UT Southwestern.

Marcus was a standout All American hurdler in the 110 meter hurdles and other track and field events. Injuries shortened his track career where he then pursued his passion for fitness and training people. Over the years he's obtained the following certifications: NESTA, ACE, NASM performance enhancement specialist/correctional exercise, CSCS.

Marcus also has been through continued education programs that are offered by Perform better. Continued education and expanding program design is what sets Marcus apart. This has led to working with many people over the years including a select few NFL clients in Damian Williams and Terence Newman just to name a few.

His experience has also led to working with Olympic track athletes preparing for the season, working with youth for different sports, rehabbing clients from their muscular deficiencies, and training the everyday human being for better functional patterns.

"It's not a hobby, it's a lifestyle"
